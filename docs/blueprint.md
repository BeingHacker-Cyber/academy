# **App Name**: AECS Scholar Hub

## Core Features:

- Intelligent Course Matchmaker Tool: An AI-driven assistant that reasons through a student's interests and future career goals to recommend the ideal IGCSE or O-Level subject combinations.
- Precision Multi-Step Enrollment: A sophisticated, step-by-step registration portal with dynamic subject selection based on grade level (6 to O-Level).
- Automated Communication System: Integrated EmailJS triggers providing instant PDF-ready receipt of registration to parents and real-time alerts to academy admin.
- Masonry Gallery & Interactive Faculty: Filterable faculty directories and a premium masonry gallery for viewing academy life and success results.
- Live Success Analytics Stats: Dynamic stats counting students and results metrics as users scroll, reinforcing academy prestige.
- AECS Brand Identity Animations: Signature logo reveal featuring SVG graduation caps and books, with staggered hero text transitions for a high-end feel.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color is a Deep Maroon (#7B1C2E), evoking tradition and academic authority. The accent color is a sophisticated Gold (#C8960C), used for call-to-action buttons to represent excellence.
- Background is an elegant, highly desaturated warm White (#F8F6F2) to ensure a clean, editorial aesthetic without being starkly modern.
- Headlines use 'Playfair Display' for its high-contrast, high-end literary feel. Body text uses 'PT Sans' for warm, humanistic readability. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Academic-themed line icons (caps, books, pens) with Navy Blue strokes to align with the Cambridge Education brand profile.
- A spacious, editorial grid with glassmorphic cards and floating geometric particles in the background for a light, multi-dimensional academic atmosphere.
- Staggered fade-up entry animations for faculty cards and masonry images to create a sense of refined, intentional growth as the user navigates.
